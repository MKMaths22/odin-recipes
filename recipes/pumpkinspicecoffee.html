Pumpkin Spice Coffee

Description

These baked scrambled eggs are light and fluffy and are a snap to put together for a big crowd. This recipe makes two pans for Christmas brunch.

More information:

  • Prep time: 5 mins
  • Cook time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup ground coffee
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons half-and-half cream, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix the ground coffee, allspice and cinnamon in a small bowl, and place into the filter of a drip coffee maker.
  2. Pour 4 cups of water into the coffee maker, and turn on the machine.
  3. When coffee has finished dripping, pour into 2 cups.
  4. Stir in cream and sugar to taste.
