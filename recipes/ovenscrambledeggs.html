Oven Scrambled Eggs

Description

These baked scrambled eggs are light and fluffy and are a snap to put together for a big crowd. This recipe makes two pans for Christmas brunch.

More information:

  • Prep time: 10 mins
  • Cook time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 12 people

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 24 eggs
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 2 1/2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Pour melted butter into a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.
  3. Whisk together eggs and salt in a large bowl until well-blended. Gradually whisk in milk.
  4. Pour egg mixture into the buttered dish.
  5. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Stir egg mixture and continue to bake until eggs are set, 10 to 15 minutes more.
